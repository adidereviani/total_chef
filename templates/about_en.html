About

Who doesn’t love salads? They’re the perfect solution for any meal – whether it’s Shabbat dinner, a barbecue, brunch, or just for dipping or eating straight from the container. They're the ideal dish for every diner.

Hi, I’m Natali Finkelstein, and this is the story of how I started my business – producing and marketing homemade-style salads.

Let’s go back to 2015, the year I participated in a show you probably know – Master Chef, Season 5. It was a long-time dream I finally achieved.

With two small children at home, I dove into an exciting television experience. After the show, I worked in the food industry, catering private events both large and small, and much more.

Then came 2020, the year of COVID, remember? With three kids and a husband whose retail business was struggling, I found myself at home, out of work, and worried about our situation.

While preparing Friday night dinner, an idea struck me – what could be better and tastier than salads for a family Friday night dinner? Tearing challah and dipping into all the dishes, not even wanting to reach the main course?

I decided to make more salads, and sell them at my husband’s store in Givatayim. With a small social media post and word-of-mouth spreading quickly, all the salads sold out within two hours.

Week by week, I made a little more, realizing that people were hungry for homemade, high-quality, hand-made salads.

At that point, my husband’s business closed, and we decided to join forces, focusing all our efforts on our new venture.

Soon after, I was fortunate enough to land on the shelves of chef Israel Aharoni and his son Uri’s new butcher shop, which had just opened on Shenkin Street in Tel Aviv.

What started as a small home business grew into a full-fledged company. I had to navigate the complex bureaucracy of the food industry, all while preserving the homemade taste and quality I hold dear.

Today, I’m proud to market to around 40 locations across the country, which I carefully select. They choose me, knowing they will receive a high-quality, exclusive, and precise brand. I consider each one part of the Total Chef family.
